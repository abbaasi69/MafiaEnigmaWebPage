Privcy Policy of Mafia Enigma


Introduction

Welcome to MafiaEnigma! We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data in an open and transparent manner. This privacy policy outlines how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information.

1. Collection of Personal Information

We collect the following types of information for specific purposes:
Phone Number: Used solely for user identification and verification. It is not visible to other users.
Username: Your chosen pseudonym for in-game identification. This is publicly visible and helps distinguish players.
Profile Picture: Optional and visible to other players during gameplay, enhancing user interaction.
Location Data: Collected to prevent multiple account usage and close proximity of players in a single game.
Game History: Includes roles, game times, etc., stored for your reference for the last 50 games. This data is private to you.
Technical Statistics: Such as user level and success rate, displayed during games to inform others of your skill level.

2. Use of Personal Information

The information collected is used to enhance your gaming experience, maintain the integrity of the game, and ensure fair play.

3. Protection of Personal Information

MafiaEnigma employs robust security measures to safeguard your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction.


Rules and Regulations
1. Account Conduct

Temporary and Permanent Blocking: Accounts violating our rules face temporary or permanent blocking. Note that coins in permanently blocked accounts are non-refundable. Reporting Violations: Users can report violations by clicking on the offender's profile image during the game.

2. In-Game Rules

Language and Behavior: Use of foul language or inappropriate voice chat is prohibited. Role Disclosure: Revealing your real role in the game is against the rules.

3. Username and Profile Picture Guidelines

Username: Should adhere to cultural norms. Avoid political, unethical, or obscure names. Farsi names are recommended. Profile Picture: Politically motivated, unethical, or racist images are not allowed. Headshot portraits are recommended due to size constraints.


Data Retention

In-Game Communications: Text and voice reports are kept for seven days, while offenders' voices are stored longer for investigations.


Questions and Contact Information

If you have any questions or need to report an error, please contact us via the email provided in the settings section.


Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We encourage you to review it regularly.



By using MafiaEnigma, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy. We are dedicated to ensuring a respectful and enjoyable gaming experience for all our users.
