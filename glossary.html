Master Mafia: Essential Communication Glossary

Accusations:
I suspect [Player] is Mafia because of [reason].
Defense:
I'm not Mafia, I was with [Player/doing an activity] at that time.
Observation:
Did anyone notice [Player] was quiet during the vote?
Alliance Building:
I trust [Player]; their actions seem honest.
Strategy Discussion:
Let’s focus on players who've been too quiet.
Role Revelation:
I'm a Villager. I have no special powers.
Voting:
I think we should vote for [Player]; their story doesn't add up.
Suspicion Expression:
Something about [Player’s] story seems off to me.
Agreement:
I agree with [Player], that's a good point.
Disagreement:
I disagree, we need more evidence before we decide.

Sus: Short for suspicious, indicating someone seems untrustworthy.
Big brain play: A smart or strategic move.
On the DL: Keeping something on the down low; secret or hidden.
Ghosting: Suddenly stopping all communication, could refer to someone being unexpectedly quiet during the game.
Spill the tea: Sharing the juicy details or secrets.
Salty: Being upset or bitter, especially after being outplayed.
No cap: No lie, telling the truth.
Flex: To show off; could be used when a player makes a good move.
Throwing shade: Subtly expressing contempt or disrespect.
Low-key: Understated or wanting to keep something quiet.
Snatched: Looking good or on point; could refer to a well-played strategy.
Yeet: To throw away; can be used when voting someone out.
Simp: Someone showing excessive sympathy or attention.
Hype: A lot of excitement and enthusiasm around something.
Squad goals: The epitome of what friends or teammates should be.
Lit: Amazing or exciting; could describe a thrilling round of the game.
Savage: Acting without regard, or brutally honest; could be a ruthless play in-game.
Woke: Being aware, could refer to being onto someone's deceptive game.
Dead: Hilariously funny; could also refer to eliminated players in a light-hearted manner.
FOMO: Fear of missing out; the anxiety that an exciting event may currently be happening elsewhere, often aroused by posts seen on social media.

Accusations:
"Your alibi doesn't add up. Explain yourself!"
"I noticed you've been overly defensive, why is that?"
"That move last night? Totally sus. Care to explain?"
Defense:
"Check my game history; I’ve been consistently loyal."
"I was busy helping the town. I have witnesses!"
"Why would I target them if I were Mafia? Think about it."
Observation:
"Isn’t it odd how quickly they jumped on that vote?"
"This person has switched sides too often. Let’s not ignore that."
"Notice how they evade direct questions about their role?"
Alliance Building:
"We think alike. Let's team up and figure this out."
"Join me next round; I believe you're innocent."
"Together, we can protect the town. Trust me."
Strategy Discussion:
"Let's keep a low profile this round and see who slips up."
"We should double-check everyone’s past actions before voting."
"Maybe we’re missing a pattern here. Let’s regroup."
Role Revelation:
"I’m the Doctor. I can prove it in the next night phase."
"Trust me, I'm the Sheriff. Here’s my evidence."
"As a Villager, I have nothing but my observations, but they’re sharp."
Voting:
"I vote we skip this round to gather more intel."
"It’s risky, but we might need to force a tie to reveal true colors."
"Are we sure? A wrong vote could cost us the game."
Suspicion Expression:
"I'm just saying, there are too many coincidences with their story."
"They’ve been right a little too often. Could be insider info."
"Something’s off. They knew too much, too soon."
Agreement:
"Exactly my thoughts! We’re on the same page."
"That’s the most logical explanation yet."
"I second that. It's the only move that makes sense now."
Disagreement:
"I see your point, but we can’t ignore the other possibilities."
"That’s a stretch, and here’s why..."
"I’m not convinced. We need a better strategy."

Accusations:
"I think you're steering the discussion. Mafia much?"
"Why so eager to vote without a proper discussion?"
"I’ve caught you in a lie; explain or face the vote."
"Your tactics scream Mafia. Got any excuses?"
"Acting too innocent. What’s your real role?"
"You’ve been manipulating the votes. Explain now!"
Defense:
"I’ve been framed! Check the facts again."
"Misunderstood again. I’m here for the town, not against it."
"I skipped last night's action for a reason. Here's why."
"You’ve got it all wrong, here’s what actually happened."
"I’m being targeted here. Don’t fall for it!"
"Listen, jumping on me without proof is what Mafia would do."
Observation:
"Two votes in a row? That pattern seems planned."
"Their silence after the accusation is telling."
"Always last to vote—strategizing much?"
"Has anyone noticed they never self-nominate?"
"Odd how you deflect questions about your night actions."
"Look who’s talking a lot today. Feeling the heat?"
Alliance Building:
"I need someone sharp. You in for a team-up?"
"Our goals align. Let’s clear out the Mafia."
"Stick with me tonight, and I’ll prove my loyalty."
"Your insights are good. Let’s collaborate."
"We’re stronger together against the Mafia."
"You call the shots, I’ll provide support."
Strategy Discussion:
"Splitting votes could reveal the Mafia’s intentions."
"Time for a silent round to see who tries to take control."
"Let’s not rush. Observing one more night could help."
"Push for answers now, they’re almost slipping."
"We need a decoy plan to catch the Mafia off guard."
"Focus on those who’ve escaped the spotlight."
Role Revelation:
"I can verify someone tonight, let’s decide who."
"I’ll show my hand: I’m the Cop, here’s the proof."
"If I reveal, I’m a target. Trust me for now."
"Revealing could be dangerous, but I’m the Doctor."
"I’m an Investigator. I know who's lying."
"Trust me as the Guardian; I protect the innocent."
Voting:
"Hold up, let’s think this through before voting."
"A strategic vote now could turn the tide."
"Consider this a tactical vote, not personal."
"Vote wisely, or we might hand the game to Mafia."
"This vote determines our fate. Choose carefully."
"Don’t follow the crowd. Think independently this round."
Suspicion Expression:
"Way too convenient, don’t you think?"
"I’m just not buying what you’re selling."
"Your story changes every round. Stick to one!"
"This is too smooth. You’re prepped for this."
"Anyone else get the feeling they’re being played?"
"They’re a bit too calm, considering the chaos."
Agreement:
"Solid point, couldn’t have said it better!"
"That lines up with what I’ve seen, too."
"Absolutely, your logic is on point."
"You’re leading us right. I’m with you."
"Right call! That’s how we win."
"I back that. It’s our best shot."
Disagreement:
"I respect your view, but here’s another angle."
"That’s one way to see it, but consider this."
"We’re not on the same page. Here’s why."
"Might be a mistake. Let’s pivot."
"Hold on, your plan has flaws."
"Not convinced. We need a backup plan."
