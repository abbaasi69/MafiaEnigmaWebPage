Roles Guidance

Villager

The Villager role is a key component of the civilian group in the Mafia game, lacking special powers but essential for game dynamics.

Villagers engage in daily discussions, using intuition and logic to identify Mafia members. Their ability to observe, persuade, and deduce is crucial for eliminating Mafia and winning the game. Although without abilities, their collective decision-making and strategizing are vital. Villagers must discern truth from deception to protect themselves and identify Mafia members. Their strength lies in numbers and the collective wisdom in discussions. Every Villager's voice and vote are crucial in unmasking and defeating the Mafia.

Mafia

Mafia members must lie throughout the game and pretend to be Villager. Mafia role is arguably the most challenging in the game, as ...

... other players may soon realize you are deceiving them. Mafia team must collaborate and think strategically during the day phase for success. At night, Mafia group must select one person to eliminate. The chosen individual, unless saved by the Doctor, will be removed from subsequent rounds of the game.

Doctor

Doctor is one of Villagers. Each night, after the Mafia chooses someone to eliminate, Doctor can attempt to save a player who they think the Mafia has targeted.

If Doctor correctly guesses and saves the right player, that player returns to the game; otherwise, the targeted player is eliminated. It's important to note that Doctor cannot save themselves two nights in a row. Therefore, if Doctor's role is revealed to Mafia, they are likely to be targeted and eliminated the following night.

Mafia Boss

The role of Mafia Boss is the leader of the Mafia group. Beyond possessing all the characteristics of a regular Mafia member, ...

... Mafia Boss has special attributes that distinguish their role. Firstly, when the Detective conducts their initial inquiry, the result for Mafia Boss will be negative, indicating that they are not a member of Mafia. Additionally, the Sniper is unable to eliminate Mafia Boss with their shot. Also, in the event of a tie in Mafia's votes during the night, Mafia Boss's vote will be the deciding factor in choosing the night's victim.

Detective

Detective is considered part of Villagers' group. Each night, after Doctor's turn, Detective can point to someone they suspect is part of Mafia.

Moderator will then inform Detective whether the chosen individual is indeed Mafia or not. The role of Detective could be considered the most intriguing in Mafia game; as they gradually uncover the identities of various players, they can subtly unite citizens against Mafia or one of its members. However, if Detective's role is revealed to Mafia, they are likely to be targeted and killed during the night! Additionally, Detective's first inquiry about Mafia Boss will return a negative result, even if Mafia Boss has been jokered.

Oracle

A member of the Mafia group in a game. In addition to the basic abilities of a regular Mafia member, the Oracle possesses a unique power ...

... that can be used twice throughout the game. The Oracle has the capability to guess the role of one of the citizens during any given day by selecting their own role. If the Oracle's guess is correct, the targeted individual will be eliminated from the game at the end of the day. However, if the Oracle's guess is incorrect, no one is eliminated. Citizens, especially the detective, must be careful not to reveal their roles, as they run the risk of being targeted and eliminated by the Oracle.

Guardian

Guardian is considered a member of the Villagers' group. As the name suggests, their armor prevents them from being killed in a daytime vote once.

After one attempt to kill Guardian during the day, their armor is considered used up and no longer functional. If Guardian is chosen to be killed in a daytime vote, they will not die, but naturally, everyone will become aware of their role. On the other hand, if Guardian is selected by Mafia to be killed at night and still has their armor, they will not die, and their role will not be revealed, as Mafia may assume the individual was a villager saved by Doctor. However, if Guardian still has their armor and Oracle correctly guesses their role, Guardian will be killed just like any other citizen if the Oracle's guess is correct.
