MafiaEnigma

Welcome to the thrilling world of "Mafia: Uncover the Truth," a captivating online game that plunges you into a realm of mystery and intrigue. In this team-based adventure, you're part of a community where distinguishing friend from foe is your ultimate challenge. The players are divided into two main factions: the stealthy Mafia and the unsuspecting Citizens.

Main Roles

Mafia

Consists of diverse roles like the cunning Mafia Boss, the mysterious Witch, the unpredictable Joker, and more. Their goal? To cleverly eliminate the Citizens without getting caught.

Villager

This group includes the vigilant Doctor, the astute Detective, the armored Knight, and other unique characters, each with a special ability. Among them lurks a Traitor, whose loyalty is a riddle itself.

Roles

Mafia

The ultimate deceivers! They must bluff, posing as innocent citizens. By night, they strategize to kill a target. Get caught lying, it's game over!

Mafia Boss

Steering the Mafia, blends in with unique traits: undetectable by the Detective's first probe and immune to the Sniper's shot. In Mafia vote ties, the Boss's decision dictates the night's outcome.

Oracle

A Mafia can guess a citizen's role twice; a correct guess eliminates the target by day's end, while a miss means no elimination. Detectives must hide their identities to avoid her deadly accuracy.

Dr. Lecter

Mafia's guardian angel with a twist! They dodge sniper bullets for their team - except for the boss, who's already bulletproof. Talk about selective saving!

Villager

The backbone of the town! With no special powers, their strength lies in keen observation and clever deduction. Can they spot the Mafia among them?

Doctor

The night-time hero guessing who to save. But beware, (s)he can't self-heal two nights straight. If Mafia guesses who, (s)he's in danger!

Detective

The one-shot wonder! (S)he can eliminate a Mafia member in a single shot, but if (s)he misses, it's game over for him/her. Caution: The Mafia Boss is bulletproof!

Guardian

An armored Villager can survive a daytime vote and a Mafia attack at night without revealing their role, yet an Oracle's correct guess can bypass the armor.

Game Play
Quick Fact

While the Citizens start the game unaware of each other's roles, the Mafia members know their allies, setting the stage for a strategic play of deception and detection.

Quick Tip

As a player, your identity is your secret weapon. Whether you're a Citizen or Mafia, revealing your role could be the game's turning point, so choose your words and actions wisely.
